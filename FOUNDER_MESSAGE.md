# Founder's Message

Welcome to EduAI-SA.

As a teacher, I have experienced the challenges facing learners, teachers, and parents every day.

I have seen learners who need more individual support than a classroom can provide. I have seen teachers spend countless hours on administration instead of focusing on teaching. I have seen parents wanting to help but not always having the tools or resources to do so.

EduAI-SA was created to help solve these real challenges.

Our mission is simple:

To give every learner a personal AI tutor and every teacher a personal AI assistant.

Technology should never replace teachers.

Instead, it should empower teachers, support parents, and help every learner reach their full potential.

This is more than software.

It is a commitment to improving education, one learner at a time.

Thank you for believing in our vision.

— Njabulo Mzulwini
Founder, EduAI-SA
